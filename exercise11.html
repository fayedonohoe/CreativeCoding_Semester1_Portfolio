Recreating a Pattern
After deciding on on mermaid scales, I began with individual shapes, repeated in a pattern.
Next, I thickened the strokes and applied a more mermaid like colour interpolation to the shapes.

I drew inspiration from a different reference to create individual squares, and rotated them 45 degrees.
From here, the squares were given rounded corners to mimick a scale.
An element of randomness was then applied to determins each shapes colour and stroke weight.
Framecount was used to change these one per second in the hope it would resemble light reflecting on a fish swimming.
